About Me

Min Key Park is a concert pianist residing in Gatineau, and has performed in many locations in Canada and the United States over the years. Studying under notable pianists such as Adrienne Shannon, David Jalbert, and Robert Palmer, he holds a Master of Music from the University of Ottawa and a Bachelor of Education from Queen’s University.

His goal as a music educator is for his students to enjoy music and to develop into proficient, independent learners. He aims to enrich the learning experience for his students by creating an environment of inspiration and safe space for learning and support.
